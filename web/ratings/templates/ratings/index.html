What is SARating?

It is a Sentiment Analysis Rating system created based on the traveler's experiences. I collected and analysed reviews from different platforms like TripAdvisor, Booking.com, and Google My Business to make a ranked list of hotels.

It aims to provide a summary and help guests to get knowledge on best services available such as hotels, restaurants, or attraction services related.

It will save their time of reading reviews across the internet before making a decision.
